## CRICKET QUIZ
**Event Rules:**
- Fun and competitive event testing IPL, Indian Cricket, and International Cricket knowledge.
- Team: 2–3 members. Inter-college teams not allowed.
- No restriction on number of teams per college.
- All teams participate in Preliminary Round; top 15 qualify for further rounds.
- Multiple rounds, formats announced on event day (audio clues, visual ID, rapid-fire, powerplay buzzer, scenario-based trivia).
- Teams eliminated after each round based on cumulative scores.
- No mobile phones, external help, or unfair means; misconduct/disqualification for rule violations.
- Quizmaster/committee decisions final.
- **Fee:** ₹200/team
- **Prize Pool:** ₹13,000 (1st: ₹1500, 2nd: ₹1000, 3rd: ₹500)
- **Delegate Pass:** Not required
- **Incharges:** Sanjai, Tholkkappiyan
