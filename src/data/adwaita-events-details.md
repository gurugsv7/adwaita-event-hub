## CULTURAL EVENTS

### SOLO SINGING - FREESTYLE
- **Time Limit:** 3 - 4 minutes
- **Registrations:** Max 5 entries/college
- **Song:** Any language or own composition
- **Karaoke/Accompaniment:** Allowed (not judged), only 1 musician, no drums
- **Submission:** Karaoke & technical requirements to incharge 1 day prior
- **Judging:** Timing, pitch/scale, vocal expression, overall impact
- **Fee:** Rs. 200/participant
- **Incharge:** Ruthramugesh

### DUET SINGING
- **Time Limit:** 4 - 5 minutes
- **Registrations:** Max 5 entries/college
- **Song:** Any language or own composition
- **Karaoke/Accompaniment:** Allowed (not judged), only 1 musician, no drums
- **Submission:** Karaoke to incharge 1 day prior
- **Judging:** Pitch/scale, timing, vocal expressions, coordination, stage presence, overall impact
- **Fee:** Rs. 300/team
- **Incharges:** Jaganathan, Rathish

### SOLO INSTRUMENTAL
- **Time Limit:** 4 minutes
- **Registrations:** Max 5 entries/college
- **Accompaniment:** Allowed (not judged), only 1 musician
- **Keyboard:** No pre-recorded beats/single-finger chords
- **Instruments:** Only 8-piece drum kit provided, bring own otherwise
- **Submission:** Requirements to incharge 1 day prior
- **Judging:** Skills, variety, creativity, innovation, overall appeal
- **Fee:** Rs. 200/participant
- **Incharges:** Cindhujaaj, Sindhuja

### BATTLE OF BANDS
- **Participants:** 4-15/team, max 9 on stage/song
- **Slots:** 7 (first come, first serve)
- **Eligibility:** Only college bands, all undergraduates, exclusive members
- **Time Limit:** 15 mins (+4 mins sound check)
- **Instruments:** Only 8-piece drum kit provided, bring own otherwise
- **Submission:** Tech rider to incharge 1 day prior
- **Judging:** Orchestration, vocal, coordination, creativity, stage presence, overall appeal
- **Fee:** Rs. 250/participant
- **Incharges:** Nishanth, Nedeunchezliyan
